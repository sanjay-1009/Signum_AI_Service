Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a claim?

Open My Policies.
Select your policy.
Click Submit Claim.
Fill the form.
Submit.

Where can I check my claim?

Dashboard
Track Claims
My Claims

Who approves claims?

Only administrators.

Can I edit my submitted claim?

No.

Can I purchase a new policy?

No.
Policies are assigned by the administrator.

Can I pay premiums?

No.
Premium payment is not available.
